## EDA

**Explanation & Tools Used**

These three visualizations, built in R using the ggplot2, scales, and dplyr libraries, provide a comprehensive analysis of state grant funding for Montgomery and Prince George’s Counties. The first chart uses a grouped bar layout to track annual funding trends, while the second employs a scatter plot with linear regression to examine the correlation between grant amounts and average wages. The final visualization focuses on FY2023 data, using a flipped bar chart and direct currency labeling to provide a high-impact, side-by-side comparison of the most recent funding levels. Together, these tools transform raw fiscal data into clear insights regarding economic investment and its relationship to local wage growth.
